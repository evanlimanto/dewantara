Project
Dewantara

We are a not-for-profit project whose goal is to help unlock the future potential of Indonesian high school students. To accomplish this, we aim to provide students with free and personalized mentorship regarding a post-secondary pathway in the USA.

Why
Dewantara?

Getting into college is difficult.

Preparing to apply to college and the actual process of applying itself are the two main challenges that every student has to grapple with.

As former high school students ourselves, we have experienced the pain of spending countless hours researching for answers, only to still be underprepared and misinformed.

About Us

We are a team of Indonesian college students who have successfully gone through the USA college application process.

We come from a variety of backgrounds, and are attending/have attended colleges all over the United States, like Berkeley, Cornell, UCLA and Yale.
